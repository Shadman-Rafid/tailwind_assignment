Mr. Dr. Md. Anwar Hossain Howladar

Secretary, Health Services Division

Dr. Md. Anwar Hossain Howlader has joined Health Services Division, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare as Secretary on 14 June, 2022.

Prior to that, he held the post of Secretary to government of the people’s republic of Bangladesh at Ministry of Women and Children Affairs and Anti-Corruption Commission (ACC), Bangladesh.
